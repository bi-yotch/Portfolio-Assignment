You see some running with the fear of ruining their makeup,
Some walk so slow as if this is where they belong.

Uncertainty
On this day.
I, alone, took a different route
Where I came across
Fallen, dead leaves scattered
Even with this mild breeze,
They lay still
Looking like clusters of countries; continents.
Some in groups, some alone.
However, none lonely. [Read more.]
